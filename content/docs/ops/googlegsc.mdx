---
title: Google Search Console
description: Google Search Console is a tool that allows you to monitor your website's performance in Google Search.
---

## Add a property

1. Click "Add a property"
2. Input your website URL
3. Click "Add"
4. Complete ownership verification

## Add a sitemap

1. Add new sitemap `https://www.[your-domain]/sitemap.xml`


---

## 添加网站

1. 在 Google Search Console 中，点击 "添加网站"
2. 输入网站的 URL
3. 点击 "添加"
4. 完成所有权验证

## 站点地图

1. 添加新的站点地图 `https://www.[your-domain]/sitemap.xml`
